Basil Chicken Recipe

Description

Basil Chicken, a culinary delight that brings together the aromatic, sweet flavor of basil with the savory taste of chicken. This classic dish, often found in Thai cuisine, is celebrated for its fragrant and delectable combination of freshly chopped basil leaves, succulent pieces of chicken, and a tantalizing sauce often crafted with soy, garlic, and a hint of spicy chilies. Saute√©d to perfection, the chicken absorbs the herbaceous essence of the basil, offering a burst of flavor with each bite. Typically served atop a bed of steaming jasmine rice, Basil Chicken is not only a feast for the palate but also a dish that entices with its aromatic allure, promising a culinary journey that is both comforting and exotic.

Ingredients

  • Ground Chicken
  • Onion
  • Green Beans
  • Sauce
  • Basil
  • Garlic Pepper
  • Rice

Steps

  1. Mince garlic and pepper.
  2. Chop onion and green beans.
  3. Heat wok over medium-high heat.
  4. Add garlic, pepper, onion, and green beans.
  5. Add ground chicken.
  6. Add sauce.
  7. Serve with rice.
